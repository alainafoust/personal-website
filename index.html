A Little about Myself

I’m interested in web development as a way to facilitate access to information and services and foster real-life connectivity. I hold a BS in Information Science from the University of Pittsburgh and have work experience as a web editor and IT data analyst. Currently, I’m employed as a pastry assistant to a James Beard Outstanding Pastry Chef nominee. What draws me to a professional career in web development is the same thing that drew me to pastry work, and to caring for houseplants and knitting clothing recreationally: my passion for watching things grow over time and ultimately bring magic to people’s lives.
